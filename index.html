05 / Tone & Voice
How We Sound
Professional but approachable. Customer-focused. Clean, not over-the-top. American English only.

Quality-focused language. No hype. No buzzwords. Let the product speak through real benefits and real reviews.

Approachable

Confident

07 / Photography
Visual Style
Clean, automotive, premium but not luxury-car-exclusive. Always show products in the context of real vehicles.

Product Shots

Clean, on white or light backgrounds. Always show seat covers installed in vehicles for context. Include close-ups of material texture and stitching.

Lifestyle Shots

Products installed in real car interiors. Show fit and finish. Use everyday trucks and SUVs, not luxury or exotic cars.

Color Variants

Individual product shots for each color with color-coded labels. 7 seat cover colors: Black, Wine Red, Beige, Black & Grey, Black & Red, Black & White, Black & Blue.

Social Content

Before/after transformation shots. Installation demos. Real customer vehicles. Short-form video dominates (TikTok, Reels).

08 / Brand Rules
Do's and Don'ts
Non-negotiable rules for anyone creating SCS content. Follow these exactly.

Always Do

  • Use Sofia Pro for all brand materials
  • Use green (#00B902) for CTA buttons
  • Show products installed in vehicles
  • Include trust badges (returns, support, designed in MN)
  • Reference Year/Make/Model vehicle compatibility
  • Say "Designed in Minnesota, USA"
  • Say "eco-conscious materials"
  • Include social proof (reviews, star ratings)
  • Write in American English
  • Use feature-benefit format with icons

Never Do

  • Never say "custom fit" or "perfect fit"
  • Never say "eco-leather"
  • Never say "Made in America/USA"
  • Never show seat covers outside of a vehicle
  • Never use fonts other than Sofia Pro
  • Never use colors outside the brand palette for CTAs
  • Never use luxury/exotic car imagery
  • Never omit trust elements from marketing
  • Never promise exact fit without the vehicle checker
  • Never use words: unlock, discover, elevate, embrace, await, enhance
